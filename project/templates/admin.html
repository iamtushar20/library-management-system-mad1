Welcome, Library Admin!

As the administrator of our library management system, you play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operations and providing a seamless experience for our users. Manage your library effectively with our comprehensive suite of tools and features.

Key Features:

Manage Books:
Add, edit, and delete books from the library catalog. Keep track of book details such as title, author, genre, and availability status.

Manage Users:
Maintain user accounts, review registration requests, and manage user privileges. Ensure a secure and user-friendly experience for all library patrons.

Book Issuance:
Process book issuance requests, track issued books, and manage due dates and returns. Keep a record of books borrowe by users for efficient library management.

Request Management:
Review and approve book requests from users. Monitor pending requests and ensure timely fulfillment to enhance user satisfaction.
